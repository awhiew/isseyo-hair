Meet Your Stylist

Hello, I'm Sunny

Isseyo Hair was born from a simple belief: that every client deserves the kind of meticulous, thoughtful care that Korean hair culture has perfected over generations. Tucked along beautiful Toorak Road in South Yarra, our salon is a calm, welcoming space where great hair happens.

As a colouring specialist, Sunny brings a refined eye for tone, dimension, and wearability. Whether you're dreaming of a subtle glow-up or a bold reinvention, she works closely with each client to deliver results that feel authentically you — and last long beyond your appointment.

Walk-ins are always welcome. Come as you are.
